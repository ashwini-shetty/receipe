Vegetable Masala Maggi Recipe | Masala Maggi Noodles

Vegetable Masala Maggi is a quick & easy Indian snack made by tossing maggi noodles along with mixed vegetables and spices. This variation of maggi noodles is healthy and a tasty twist to the simple & plain version due to nutritive value of fresh vegetables added to this dish. The veggies are saut√©ed in a pan along with the maggi noodles which imparts a unique taste to the noodles as well.

prep time

5 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

steps to be followed

Heat oil in a pan and add finely chopped onions to it. Also add some finely chopped green chillies. Fry the onion for 2 minutes as it starts browning. Add chopped carrots, capsicum and peas to the pan. Cook the vegetables for 2-3 minutes.Add chopped tomatoes to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes till the tomatoes become soft. Then add salt, red chilli powder, turmeric powder (haldi) and chaat masala. 1/2 Teaspoon Turmeric Powder (Haldi),1/2 Teaspoon Red Chilli Powder,1/2 Teaspoon Chaat Masala. Also add 2 packets of maggi tastemaker masala. Mix well and roast the spices for couple of minutes. Add 2 cups of water to the pan and bring it to a boil. Then add the maggi noodles to the pan and boil for 3-4 minutes. Stir the noodles occasionally and cook till all the water is absorbed. Vegetable masala maggi is ready. Serve it hot.

ingredients

2 Packets Maggi Noodles

1 Small Onion

1/3 Cup Carrot (Gajar) chopped

1/3 Cup Green Capsicum (Bell Pepper / Shimla Mirch) chopped

1/3 Cup Green Peas (Matar)

1 Large Tomatoes

1/2 Teaspoon Turmeric Powder (Haldi)

1/2 Teaspoon Red Chilli Powder

1/2 Teaspoon Chaat Masala

1/4 Teaspoon Salt or to taste

1/2 Tablespoon Cooking Oil
